4. Extraction Notes

  • Cropped title: The chart title is partially cut off at the top of the image and could not be fully read.
  • Red dot artifact: A small red dot appears near year 2020 at approximately y = 350. This does not correspond to any bar top and is likely a plotting artifact or stray annotation. It was not included in the extracted data.
  • Short bars (2011–2012 red): The Solvent Extraction bars for 2011 (~35) and 2012 (~45) are quite short relative to the y-axis range, making precise height estimation harder. Confidence is ±5–10 papers for these values.
  • 2019 vs 2020 Solvent Extraction: The red bars for 2019 (~175) and 2020 (~170) appear to dip from the 2018 peak (~210). This is a notable trend break — the Solvent Extraction count decreases while Separation continues to grow. Readings are moderate confidence (±10).
  • General precision: Without gridlines, all bar heights were estimated by interpolating between y-axis tick marks spaced at 100-unit intervals. Typical uncertainty is ±10–15 papers for taller bars and ±5–10 for shorter bars.
